Reasoning

We chose Roboto as our typeface for its exceptional readability, versatility to switch between multiple devices, and its clean, modern feel. Roboto falls under the Neo-Grotesque category of typefaces, characterized by geometric shapes with open curves. Neo-Grotesque are widely considered to be very readable, making Roboto a great choice for accessibility. Robotoâ€™s versatility, wide selection of fonts
