Logo

Reasoning

Our logo is a stack of folded clothes. Since our app helps people create outfits a stack of clothes is good. We decided to try and use items that would relate to our target market which is generally men, ex. Jeans, shirt with collar, etc. while also keeping the design simple and recognizable.

Typography

M PLUS Rounded 1c Bold 700

The font that we used for the logo is M PLUS Rounded 1c Bold. We chose this font because it is rounded like the logo, but also looks professional and modern.
